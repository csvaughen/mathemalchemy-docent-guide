# Frieze Groups

**In the exhibit:** [[Frieze Tables]] · related: [[Quiltlet]]

A **frieze pattern** repeats infinitely in exactly one direction, like a decorative border. There are exactly **7 frieze groups**, generated by combinations of translation, horizontal/vertical reflection, 180° rotation, and glide reflection. (Informal names help on tours: hop, step, sidle, spinning hop, spinning sidle, jump, spinning jump.)

**One-liner for tours:** "Millions of artists across history, and only 7 possible border designs — symmetry is that strict."

**Activity idea:** the scavenger hunt from [[Frieze Tables]] works anywhere on campus — corridors, brickwork, sweater cuffs.
