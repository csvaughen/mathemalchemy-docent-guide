# Polyhedra and Euler's Formula

**In the exhibit:** [[The Curio Shop]]

The zoo of 3D shapes with flat faces: 5 Platonic solids (identical regular faces, identical vertices), 13 Archimedean solids, and exactly **92 Johnson solids** (strictly convex, regular polygon faces, not uniform — completely classified in 1969).

**Euler's formula** rules them all: **V − E + F = 2** for any convex polyhedron. It's secretly [[Topology]] — the "2" is the Euler characteristic of the sphere.

**One-liner for tours:** "Every solid shape in the universe obeys one equation: corners minus edges plus faces equals two."
