# Trigonometry and Navigation

**In the exhibit:** [[The Lighthouse]]

Triangles as measuring instruments: knowing one side and two angles determines everything else (Law of Sines/Cosines). **Triangulation** located ships from lighthouses centuries before GPS did the same with satellites and **spherical coordinates** — 3D addresses on a curved Earth. **Wave propagation** describes how the lighthouse's light (or a satellite's laser) travels.

**One-liner for tours:** "GPS is trigonometry with better hardware."

**Classroom tie-in (MCCC):** precalc/trig courses — the most directly course-aligned scene for intro students.
